About Me

I am Walter Omwangu, a cybersecurity and network security professional based in Nairobi, Kenya, with hands-on experience securing enterprise environments through security monitoring, incident response, infrastructure hardening, and proactive threat detection. My technical background spans SIEM monitoring, firewall administration, threat intelligence, vulnerability management, access control, and enterprise security operations.

Currently working as an Information System Security Officer, I support enterprise security initiatives by monitoring security events, strengthening access controls, conducting risk assessments, improving compliance posture, and enhancing infrastructure resilience against evolving cyber threats. My work includes security operations monitoring, firewall log analysis, ISO 27001 compliance monitoring, vulnerability remediation, and supporting business continuity through secure system design.

Beyond operations, I actively build and maintain a cybersecurity home lab environment where I simulate enterprise networks, perform attack simulations, develop detection use cases, and strengthen incident investigation capabilities using industry-standard tools and frameworks. I also serve as a Cisco Networking Academy Instructor, mentoring future technology professionals and contributing to cybersecurity awareness, technical training, and practical skills development.
